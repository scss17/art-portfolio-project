Maria Campos

Architect, Artist

Born in 1998 in Durango, Mexico. Maria Campos is a Mexican artist currently living in British Columbia, Canada, since 2022. Maria earned her Bachelor of Architecture from the Monterrey Institute of Technology and Higher Education in 2021. Before graduating, she gained international experience in La Coru√±a, Spain. Additionally, she had the opportunity to complete an internship at INTERCERAMIC. This company manufactures ceramic and porcelain tiles for floors and walls, where she was involved in the design and construction of store layouts.

In 2022, Maria moved to Canada to expand her skill set by studying for a diploma in Visual Arts at Camosun College. She is expected to graduate in 2025. Maria has participated in several art exhibitions, including "Out of the Grey," "Library Mezzanine," "Brainstorm," and "Narrative to Reality." This year, she will be featured in the Grad Show Exhibition, showcasing the work of visual arts graduates at Camosun.
